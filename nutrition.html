Nutritional Counseling

Achieve Fitness now offers nutritional counseling to non-members.  The benefits of nutritional counseling are to help individuals make and maintain dietary changes, whether it’s for weight loss, disease prevention, or overall health.  Your nutritional counselor works with you in the following ways:

  • Assess current diet and address key changes

  • Education of healthy eating habits

  • Identify bad habits and learn how to create better habits

  • Learn how to avoid triggers
  • Track food through journaling or apps
  • Meal prep ideas
  • Macronutrient based programs
  • Support to maintain and live a healthier life

Package Options

Appointments are 30 minute private sessions.  Options for nutritional counseling:


Individual sessions - $50/session

Weekly session packages - choose from 1 month (4 sessions), 3 months (13 sessions), 6 months (26 sessions), 9 months(39 sessions), or 12 months (52 sessions).  Session packages start as low as $26/session.

Bi-weekly session packages - choose from 1 month (2 sessions), 3 months (6 sessions), 6 months (13 sessions), 9 months (19 sessions), or 12 months (26 sessions).  Session packages start as low as $36/session.
