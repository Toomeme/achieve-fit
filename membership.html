Personal monthly membership

Choose a 3-, 6-, 9-, or 12 month membership then choose to train 2 or 3 times per week.  Personal training memberships start as low as $32/session.

Partner membership

Don’t want to train alone?  Want to help motivate a friend?  Want to save money?  Then a partner membership is for you.  Options are the same as a personal membership.  Partner training memberships start as low as $23/session per person.

 

Group training membership

Have a group of friends or family members that you want to work out with?  Looking to join a group for some personal training?  Bring your own group of 3-4 people or join our scheduled group training classes.  Private group training memberships start as low as $15/session per person.  Join our scheduled group training classes and come to as many as you’d like for $150/month.
